## 🚀 Business Growth Opportunities

### 1. Increase Revenue from Top Categories

Focus marketing campaigns on **Electronics**, because it is the strongest revenue category.

### 2. Protect Top Products from Stockouts

Maintain inventory for high-performing products like **Smartphones**.

### 3. Improve Customer Retention

Send personalized offers, reminder campaigns, and discounts to **churn-risk customers**.

### 4. Increase Average Order Value

Create bundle offers such as:

> **Smartphone + Headphones**
> **Notebook + Pen Combo**
> **Yoga Mat + Fitness Accessories**

### 5. Use Customer Segments for Marketing

Do not send the same offer to everyone. Use different offers for **VIP**, **Loyal**, **Regular**, and **At Risk** customers.
